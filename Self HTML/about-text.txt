Self is an acronym for Software Engineering Learning Framework.
As our name implies, we are a platform that teaches software 
engineering in a fun and engaging manner.  At Self, we believe in 
the gamification of education for a more appealing and hands-on 
experience. We go beyond the traditional teaching methods, as our 
priority is to motivate students to learn more effectively. With the 
help of our learning resources, you can enjoy self-learning at your 
own pace.
